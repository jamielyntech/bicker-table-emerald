Most organizations can name the change. Few can name why the gap exists, or what's actually creating it. OOSI is built to answer that second question, using evidence, not opinions.

Our Philosophy

The evidence becomes the assessment.

We don't start with a questionnaire, and we don't start with a solution. We start with your business question, and with how your organization already operates, documented in the systems where the real work happens.

Interviews and surveys tell us what people believe happened. Documentary evidence tells us what actually did.

Policies, SOPs, org charts, governance records, project documentation, wherever it already lives, Google Drive, SharePoint, Confluence, Notion. We investigate documentary evidence first. Interviews get added later, only if the evidence calls for it.

What this investigation is not
  • Not a full organizational audit. The evidence reviewed serves your one business question, not an exhaustive review of everything in your company.
  • Not certification preparation. Readiness for SOC 2, ISO 42001, or the EU AI Act is a separate, broader engagement, scoped only once it's the right next step.
  • Not a recommendation to implement. OOSI identifies the gap and what's creating it. Closing it is a decision, and a project, that comes after.
